# AI Personalization Specialist

This certification demonstrates that you can design and operate AI-powered personalization systems that produce genuine, context-aware outreach at scale — not mail merge, not personalization theater.

## What This Certification Covers

- Personalization principles and the spectrum from Level 0 to Level 4
- Prompt engineering for outreach: structure, components, testing methodology
- Multi-layer personalization: company signal + role challenge + individual hook
- LinkedIn research automation at scale
- Vertical and industry-specific messaging frameworks
- QA infrastructure: automated checks, batch review, auto-retry
- Live personalized campaign execution and hypothesis testing

## How to Earn It

Complete all modules in the AI Personalization track (AP-1 through AP-6) and pass the AP-7 capstone, which includes a live campaign run and personalization depth hypothesis test.
