# GTM Architect Track

The previous tracks taught you components. This track teaches you systems.

A GTM Architect can look at a company's growth stage, market position, and team structure — and design a complete, integrated GTM motion from scratch. They know which data sources to connect, which signals to watch, how to route prospects through enrichment and scoring, how to personalize at scale, and how to measure what's working.

This is the track that moves you from "I know how to use Bitscale" to "I can design GTM infrastructure."
