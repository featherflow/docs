## How It Works

The GTM Skill Tree has three tiers. You move through them in order —
Foundation unlocks the specialist branches, and completing two specialist
branches unlocks the GTM Architect master certification.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tier 0: GTM Foundation (Required for everyone)">
    **5 modules · ~2 hours · Earns: Foundation Badge**

    Covers why GTM automation matters, how to define a tight ICP, how to create
    your first Bitscale grid, how to interpret enrichment data, and the core
    workflow model that underpins everything else in the platform.

    [Start Foundation →](/academy/foundation/what-is-gtm-automation)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tier 1: Specialist Tracks (Choose your path)">
    **7–8 modules per track · ~5–6 hours · Earns: Specialist Certificate**

    Pick the track that matches your role and goals. You can complete more
    than one — each earns its own certificate and counts toward GTM Architect.

    | Track | Best for | Status |
    |---|---|---|
    | [Outbound Automation](/academy/outbound/overview) | SDRs, Founders, Agencies | Available now |
    | [Data & Enrichment](/academy/enrichment/overview) | RevOps, Data teams | Launching Week 2 |
    | [Signal Intelligence](/academy/signals/overview) | Growth, Senior ICs | Launching Week 3 |
    | [AI Personalization](/academy/ai/overview) | Marketers, Power users | Launching Week 3 |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tier 2: GTM Architect (Master certification)">
    **6 modules · ~6 hours · Earns: GTM Architect Master Certificate**

    Unlocks after completing Foundation + any 2 specialist tracks.
    Covers full-stack GTM system design, team-level Bitscale deployment,
    advanced API architecture, and a live capstone review.

    [View GTM Architect →](/academy/architect/overview)
  </Step>
</Steps>
