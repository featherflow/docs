# Your GTM Journey

Bitscale Academy rewards doing the work, not just watching the video. Every module has an XP value. Complete the hands-on challenge, submit the form, earn the XP. Hit the track threshold, take the certification assessment, earn the badge.

The badge is LinkedIn-shareable and Accredible-verified. It tells the world you can build GTM systems that work in production — not just talk about them.

<Info>
  **XP is earned, not given.** Reading a module earns you 0 XP. Completing the hands-on challenge earns you 100–300 XP depending on the module. The certification assessment is designed to catch anyone who skipped the work.
</Info>

## How to Claim XP

1. Complete the hands-on challenge at the end of each module
2. Submit via the form linked at the bottom of each challenge section
3. Your submission is logged — we review and verify
4. Once you hit the certification threshold, you'll receive an email invite to take the assessment
