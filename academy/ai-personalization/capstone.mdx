This is the capstone for the AI Personalization track. You're building a fully personalized outbound campaign — multi-layer personalization, QA infrastructure, live sends, results tracking.

---

## What You're Building

A production-ready personalized outbound campaign with:

1. **Prospect grid** — 50+ contacts from a defined ICP
2. **Multi-layer personalization** — company signal + role challenge + individual hook
3. **Full prompt system** — with vertical-specific variants
4. **QA infrastructure** — all 4 checks + master status + auto-retry
5. **Live sends** — at least 40 approved emails sent via sequencer
6. **Results tracking** — reply sentiment, personalization level correlation with reply rate
7. **Capstone analysis** — did personalization depth correlate with reply rate?

---

## Build Instructions

**Step 1: Source and enrich (45 min)**
50+ contacts with full enrichment: firmographics, funding, tech stack, signal, LinkedIn research.

**Step 2: Multi-layer personalization (60 min)**
All three layers for every contact:
- Company context synthesis column
- Role challenge column
- Individual observation column
- Personalization classification (full / two-layer / etc.)

**Step 3: Prompt system (30 min)**
Build prompt variants:
- Full personalization prompt (Layer 1 + 2 + 3)
- Two-layer prompt (Layer 1 + 2, no individual signal)
- Company-only prompt (Layer 1 only)
- Vertical-specific prompt (for your primary vertical)

**Step 4: Generate and QA (30 min)**
- Run all 4 QA checks
- Master QA status
- Auto-retry for failures
- Manual review of flagged rows

**Step 5: Send (ongoing over 1 week)**
- Export approved rows to sequencer
- Send minimum 40 emails
- Track replies in a results grid

**Step 6: Analyze (30 min)**
- Tag each reply with sentiment
- Calculate reply rate by personalization level
- Did higher personalization = higher reply rate?

---

## Capstone Hypothesis

Every submission must include a hypothesis test:

*"Contacts with full three-layer personalization will have a higher reply rate than contacts with two-layer or company-only personalization."*

Test this against your actual data. Document:
- Reply rate by personalization level (full / two-layer / company-only)
- Whether the hypothesis was confirmed, partially confirmed, or rejected
- What this suggests for your future workflow

Pass this capstone to earn the **AI Personalization Specialist** certification.

Requirements for passing:
