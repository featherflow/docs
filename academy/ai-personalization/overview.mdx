# AI Personalization Track

Personalization isn't inserting `{{first_name}}` into an email template. That's mail merge. Personalization is demonstrating that you understand someone's specific context — their role, their challenges, their company's current moment — and connecting that to something genuinely relevant.

The problem has always been scale. Real personalization took too long. Now it doesn't.
