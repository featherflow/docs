## The Capstone

To earn your **Bitscale Outbound Automation Specialist Certificate**, complete
the following project and submit it for review.

## What to Build

Build and launch a 50-contact outbound campaign from scratch using Bitscale.
Your submission should include:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define your ICP and build your list">
    Use Bitscale's Find Companies or Find People sources to build a list of
    50 contacts that match a specific ICP. Document your filter criteria.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enrich your contacts">
    Run email and/or phone waterfall enrichment. Achieve at least 70% email
    coverage on your list. Document your fill rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate personalized copy">
    Use Bitscale's AI columns to generate personalized email copy for each
    contact. At least one personalization variable must come from an
    enrichment column (not manually written).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up and launch the sequence">
    Export to your sending tool and configure a 3-touch sequence.
    Include a screenshot of the campaign setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your reflection (200 words)">
    What targeting logic did you use? What personalization hook did you choose
    and why? What would you do differently next time?
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Submit

<Card title="Submit Your Capstone →" icon="paper-plane" href="https://bitscale.fillout.com/academy-capstone-outbound">
  Upload your screenshots and reflection. Review takes 2-3 business days.
  You'll receive your certificate via email with a LinkedIn-shareable badge link.
</Card>

<Note>
  Capstone reviews are done by the Bitscale team. We'll give you specific
  feedback regardless of whether you pass on the first submission.
</Note>
