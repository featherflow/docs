## The GTM Skill Tree

Start with the Foundation track — it's required and takes about 2 hours.
Then choose the specialist branch that matches your role and goals.
Complete two or more specialist tracks to unlock the GTM Architect master certification.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="GTM Foundation" icon="seedling" href="/academy/foundation/what-is-gtm-automation">
    **Start here.** 5 modules · ~2 hours · Required for all tracks.

    and the Bitscale workflow model.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outbound Automation" icon="paper-plane" href="/academy/outbound/overview">
