# Data & Enrichment Track

Outbound Automation was the delivery system. Data & Enrichment is the intelligence layer that makes everything it delivers actually relevant.

Bad data costs more than people think. A rep spending 20 minutes researching a prospect before a call that never should have been made is a real cost — it's just distributed and invisible. At scale, bad data means wasted enrichment credits, burned sender reputation on wrong-fit prospects, and sequences that perform poorly because they're targeting the wrong people.

This track fixes the intelligence layer.
