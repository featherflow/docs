# Data & Enrichment Specialist

This certification demonstrates that you can build production-grade data pipelines in Bitscale — from source to enrichment to scoring — with the data quality and coverage needed to power real outbound programs.

## What This Certification Covers

- Multi-source data acquisition and quality evaluation
- Waterfall enrichment for maximum coverage and credit efficiency
- Data cleaning at scale (normalization, deduplication, freshness)
- Company intelligence: firmographics, funding signals, tech stack, activity
- Contact scoring models combining fit and timing signals
- Technographic analysis and competitive displacement identification
- Intent data: first-party and third-party signal activation

## How to Earn It

Complete all modules in the Data & Enrichment track (DE-1 through DE-7) and pass the DE-8 capstone with a score of 70% or higher.

## Badge

Upon passing, you'll receive an Accredible-verified digital badge. Share it on LinkedIn or add it to your resume. The badge links to a verified record of your capstone submission and pass score.

<Info>
  **Review timeline:** Capstone submissions are reviewed within 3 business days. You'll receive an email with your score and feedback.
</Info>
