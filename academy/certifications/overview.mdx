Each certification requires completing a full track plus a hands-on capstone project.
All certificates are issued as verifiable digital badges you can add directly
to your LinkedIn profile.

## How digital badges work

All certificates are issued via [Accredible](https://www.accredible.com).
When you earn a certificate you'll receive an email with:
- A shareable certificate URL
- A **LinkedIn "Add to Profile"** button that auto-fills your credential
- A downloadable PDF certificate

Employers and clients can verify any Bitscale certificate at any time.
