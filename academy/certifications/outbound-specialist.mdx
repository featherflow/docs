## About This Certificate

The Outbound Automation Specialist certificate confirms you can build,
run, and optimize personalized multi-channel outbound campaigns using Bitscale.
It's designed for SDRs, founders, and agency operators.

## Requirements

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the GTM Foundation track">
    The [Foundation Badge](/academy/certifications/foundation) is required
    before starting any specialist track.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete OA-1 through OA-6">
    Work through all 6 Outbound Automation modules in order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the OA-7 Capstone Project">
    Build and launch a real 50-contact outbound campaign.
    Submit screenshots and a 200-word reflection.
    Review takes 2-3 business days.
  </Step>
</Steps>
