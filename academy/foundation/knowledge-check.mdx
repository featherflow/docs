# Foundation Knowledge Check

You've covered five modules. Now let's find out how much actually stuck.

The knowledge check is 15 questions covering all five Foundation modules. You need 80% to pass (12/15 correct). If you don't pass on the first attempt, review the modules you struggled on and retake it — there's no limit on attempts.

<Warning>
  **Don't rush this.** The knowledge check is also your first gate to Foundation Certification. The certification assessment is harder. Treat this as preparation, not just a box-tick.
</Warning>

---

## What's Covered

| Module | Topics Tested |
|--------|--------------|
| F-1: What is GTM Automation? | GEO framework, four Bitscale layers, real cost of manual GTM |
| F-2: Understanding Your ICP | Four ICP dimensions, how they map to Bitscale columns, calibration loop |
| F-3: Your First Grid | Grid creation, column types, import, run order, expected outputs |
| F-4: Reading Your Data | Confidence scores, freshness windows, catch-all domains, cascade enrichment |
| F-5: Bitscale Workflow Model | Sources, run conditions, scheduling, export filters |

---

## Unlock on Pass

If you pass (≥ 80%), you earn:
- **+200 XP** (bringing your Foundation total to 750 XP)
- **Eligibility for Foundation Certification** — you'll receive a separate invite to take the certification assessment
- **Access to the Outbound Automation track**

<Tip>
  **Founding Cohort:** The first 50 people to complete Foundation Certification (not just the knowledge check) receive a hand-signed certificate, a Hall of Fame feature, and early access to all future tracks.

  [Reserve your founding cohort spot →](https://bitscale.fillout.com/academy-founding)
</Tip>
