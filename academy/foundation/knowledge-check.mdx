## Ready to earn your Foundation Badge?

You've completed all 5 Foundation modules. The knowledge check is 10 questions
covering the core concepts from F-1 through F-5. Passing score is 8/10.

Once you pass, you'll receive your **Foundation Badge** — a digital credential
you can add directly to your LinkedIn profile.

<Card title="Take the Knowledge Check →" icon="clipboard-check" href="https://bitscale.fillout.com/academy-foundation-check">
  10 questions · ~5 minutes · Passing score: 80%
</Card>

<Note>
  You can retake the check as many times as you need. There's no penalty
  for retrying — the goal is that you actually understand the material,
  not that you pass on the first attempt.
</Note>

## What's covered

| Module | Topics tested |
|---|---|
| F-1: What is GTM Automation? | GEO framework, the four GTM layers, why manual GTM doesn't scale |
| F-2: Understanding Your ICP | ICP dimensions, Bitscale parameters, specificity vs. breadth trade-off |
| F-3: Your First Grid | Grid structure, column types, import methods, first enrichment run |
| F-4: Reading Your Data | Fill rates, catch-all detection, data freshness, confidence indicators |
| F-5: The Bitscale Workflow Model | Sources, columns, run conditions, scheduling, export destinations |
