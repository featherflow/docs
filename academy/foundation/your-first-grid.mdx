## What is a Grid?

A grid is Bitscale's core building block. Think of it as a spreadsheet that
runs workflows — each row is a company or contact, each column is either data
you're adding or an action you're running. The grid is where everything happens:
enrichment, research, personalization, and export.

## Creating Your First Grid

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Grids page">
    From the Bitscale sidebar, click **Grids** → **New Grid**.
    Give it a name that describes what it's for (e.g., "ICP Companies — April").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your columns">
    Start with the basics: Company Name, Website, LinkedIn URL.
    These are your identifiers — everything else gets enriched from them.
    Click **+ Add Column** → choose **Text** for each of these.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your first rows">
    Click **Import** → upload a CSV with 10-20 companies from your ICP.
    If you don't have a list yet, use the **Find Companies** source
    to generate one based on your ICP parameters from F-2.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first enrichment column">
    Click **+ Add Column** → search for **LinkedIn Company Enrichment**.
    Map the LinkedIn URL column as the input. Run it on your first 5 rows.
    Watch what comes back.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the output">
    You should now see headcount, description, industry, and employee count
    populated for each company. This is the foundation of every workflow
    you'll build on Bitscale.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your Challenge

<Card title="Before moving to F-4" icon="flag-checkered">
  Build a grid with at least 10 rows from your ICP. Add 3 columns:
  Company Name, Website, and LinkedIn URL. Run the LinkedIn Company
  Enrichment on all 10 rows. Take note of which fields come back —
  you'll need them in the next module.
</Card>

<Tip>
  Use the **Templates** section to clone a pre-built grid if you want
  a head start. The "ICP Company Research" template is a good starting point.
</Tip>
