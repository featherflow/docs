## Post Sign-up

Once you have signed-up on the platform, you would be taken to the **'Grids'** Page where you can create custom sheets (we call it grids) for your lead generation and enichment usecases.

You can explore the **'Templates'** Page to help you get started. They contain the common usecases, templatized with all the relvant Actions defined and setup already.

Just Clone it, Edit it and Make it your own!
